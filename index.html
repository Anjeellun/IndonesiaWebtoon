Dedes

Isekai based on an Indonesian historical story! A student awakens in the body of Dedes, a girl who will become the mother of the Javanese kings. The longer he was in Dedes' body, the more he realized that the existing history was not entirely accurate.

The Lost Harmony

Ellie is the daughter of a famous musician family. But she has a flaw, she is terrible at music. This causes Ellie to have such a difficult childhood. Until one day, Ellie realizes she can communicate with a ghost named Ethan, a famous musician. What does Ethan expect from Ellie? How is their relationship going?

Kosan 95

It's wrong to think Kosan 95 is an usual boarding house. Whoever accepts the invitation and obtains a room, the host will cover your living expenses, but at what cost? Rena is a regular girl who receives an invitation to Kosan 95. What is the mystery in that boarding house?
